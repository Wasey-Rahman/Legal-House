Business Law

Business law, often known as commercial law or mercantile law, is the set of regulations controlling inter-person interactions in commercial concerns, whether established by convention, agreement, national or international legislation.

Tax Law

Tax law, a set of rules by which a public authority has a right over taxpayers, forcing them to transfer to the authority a portion of their income or assets. The power to impose taxes is generally recognized as a government right.

Education Law

An education system's operations are governed by a series of laws called education laws. To develop complete educational plans for a particular country, several countries have intricate legal systems governing education.
